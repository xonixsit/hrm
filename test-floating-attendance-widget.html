Floating Attendance Widget Demo

Features

  • Always Accessible: Appears on every page in the bottom-right corner
  • Compact Design: Shows current status and timer in minimal space
  • Quick Actions: Clock in/out and break management without navigation
  • Real-time Updates: Timer updates automatically every minute
  • Mobile Friendly: Responsive design works on all devices

How to Use

  1. Click the floating button in the bottom-right corner
  2. Use "Clock In" to start your work session
  3. Use "Break" to take a break (timer switches to break duration)
  4. Use "Clock Out" to end your work day
  5. Click outside or the X button to collapse the widget

Status Indicators

Working (Green)
On Break (Yellow)
Clocked Out (Gray)

This floating widget will appear on every page once integrated into the AuthenticatedLayout. It provides quick access to attendance tracking without taking up valuable screen real estate.
